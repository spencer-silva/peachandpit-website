How it works

It's simple.

Once a day, users share their Peach (best part) and their Pit (worst part) — then rate their day on a scale from 1 to 5.

1
Share your Peach 🍑 What was the best part of your day?
2
Share your Pit 🪨 What was the hardest part of your day?
3
Rate your day Score it 1–5 and see how your week looked over time.
